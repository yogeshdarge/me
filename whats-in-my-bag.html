What's in my bag?

The software I use, the gadgets I like, and the other things I recommend. Here's a great list of all my favorite products.

  • Material

  • Apple Airpods 4

    It's probably the object I use the most after my phone and laptop. I use it for everything, listening to music or taking calls. They are very convenient and the sound quality is excellent.

  • Apple iPhone 16Pro

    I am not used to investing and updating my phone frequently, I used my last iphone 11Pro for 4 years, I will use this one for 3+ years as well.

  • Apple Macbook Pro M1

    My MacBook Pro M1 is the heart of my workflow — I use it daily for everything from mobile app development and UI/UX design to creative work in Adobe and video editing tools. It’s fast, silent, and incredibly reliable.

  • Apple Watch Series 7

    I use it mainly to keep track of my workouts

  • Dell Inspiron 14 (5410) 2-in-1

    My Dell 2-in-1 touchscreen laptop is perfect for quick UI sketching, on-the-go development, and responsive design testing. Its flexibility complements my main setup effortlessly.
  • Software

  • Visual Studio Code

    My primary code editor for mobile, web, and backend development. It’s fast, lightweight, and highly customizable — perfect for writing clean, structured code across multiple languages and frameworks.

  • Adobe Photoshop

    My go-to tool for UI design, image editing, and creative assets. I use it regularly to design app screens, social media creatives, and marketing visuals with precision and consistency.

  • Adobe Illustrator

    Used for creating vector-based graphics, logos, icons, and scalable UI elements. It’s essential in my workflow for clean, precise design assets that work across mobile, web, and print platforms.

  • TablePlus

    A fast, modern database client I use for managing MySQL, PostgreSQL, and SQLite databases. It offers a clean UI for writing queries, browsing data, and managing schemas efficiently — essential for full-stack development and quick debugging.
